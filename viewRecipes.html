Greek Yoghurt Bowl

Back

Recipe Overview

Likes: 72

Category: Breakfast

Description: A creamy Greek yoghurt bowl topped with fresh fruits, chia seeds, and honey. A perfect healthy breakfast full of nutrients and flavor.

Recipe Creator

Hanan
Recipe Creator

Ingredients

  • Greek yoghurt — 1 cup
  • Fresh fruits — mixed berries
  • Chia seeds — 1 tsp
  • Honey — 1 tsp

Preparation Steps

Quick and easy steps.

  1. Place Greek yoghurt in a bowl.
  2. Add fresh fruits on top.
  3. Sprinkle chia seeds evenly.
  4. Drizzle honey over the bowl.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy.
